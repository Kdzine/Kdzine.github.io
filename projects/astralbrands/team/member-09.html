Director of Product Development

Rachel Sullivan

Rachel is an accomplished R&D chemist with a diverse background and unique skill set that bridges the gap between science and marketing. Rachel joined ASTRAL HEALTH & BEAUTY in 2015 to drive innovation and boost speed-to-market success for all three of ASTRAL’s brands. She received her Bachelor of Science degree in Chemistry from Emory University and has created reactive materials for the Department of Defense, patented novel encapsulation techniques for active ingredients in the medical and cosmetic industries, and has formulated advanced anti-aging skin care products and color cosmetics.
