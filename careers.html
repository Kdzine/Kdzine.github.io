Astral Careers

One Growing Family. Three Life-Enriching Brands.

Join us at a company where the entrepreneurial spirit is valued, encouraged and celebrated! Astral Health & Beauty is a natural choice for people who add a dash of passion to everything they do. Our three lifestyle brands, Aloette, CosMedix and PÜR Cosmetics, provide opportunities to bring beauty products of exceptional quality and value to consumers worldwide.

  • our pillars of strength: To shape & guide our brands and the people who manage them:
  • We deliver memorable, life-enriching experiences through beauty.
  • We combine scientifically advanced technology with nature-based ingredients.
  • We are environmentally conscientious and advocate cruelty-free products.
  • We support philanthropic organizations because we believe that people make life beautiful.
  • Guiding Principles: Shaping The Way We Do Business
  • Allocate resources wisely, always asking, “Is the investment worth the return?”
  • Hold ourselves accountable, realizing every employee has an impact
  • Coach one another, helping team members find their fastball
  • Think big; be an innovator and a creator
  • Take chances; learn from your mistakes
  • Agree to disagree, but never be disagreeable
  • Treat every day as if it were opening day
  • Never, ever give up
  • We are three brands, but one family

Innovate with Us!

Channel your entrepreneurial energies into a career with Astral Health & Beauty. No matter which of our three prestigious brands you choose to build your future with, you can expect to learn, grow and work with great people!

Astral is redefining the face of beauty with innovative and transformative products. We believe diversity and collaboration are essential to developing products that meet the needs of a global marketplace. Our success is the result of entrepreneurial spirit, pushing the boundaries, and knowing that our commitment to the customer is more than skin deep. Join us!
