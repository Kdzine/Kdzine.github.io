COSMEDIX Global Brand Director

Christine Hayes

Christine began her career with L’Oreal’s Marketing TRACK Brand Management Development Program where she gained exposure and experience in market research, sales, finance, and public relations. Christine then transitioned to Newell Rubbermaid working on the Beauty and Style business for the Goody brand where she managed the Styling and Hair Accessories platform. In this role, she managed the core product development and travelled to Asia quarterly to ensure best design and cost. Most recently to joining Astral, she has been leading and developing the global relaunch strategy for the Paper Mate brand by optimizing positioning, portfolio mix and managing the innovation funnel.
