National Sales Director, PÜR Cosmetics

Heidi Davis

Heidi brings to Pur Cosmetics over 25 years of prestige beauty and skin care experience. Prior to joining the Astral Family, Heidi has acted as the Regional Sales Manager for Stila Cosmetics, the National Sales and Education Manager for Dr Susan Taylor’s Rx and the National Sales Director for Urban Decay Cosmetics. Heidi utilizes her vast experience with such retailers as Ulta, Sephora, Nordstrom, Macys, and Saks Fifth Avenue to bring new and innovative ideas to her team and assist in growing the business. With PÜR Cosmetics, Heidi oversees our team of Regional Sales Managers, Account Executives, and our extensive artistry team across the country.
