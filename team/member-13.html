E-commerce Manager

Emily Foster

Emily earned her Bachelors of Science degree in Business Management from Georgia Institute of Technology. Her first role post-graduation was with a family-owned, multi-brand footwear manufacturer, where she gained experience in email marketing, site merchandising, and re-platforming the brand’s e-commerce website to Amazon’s webstore. At Astral Brands, Emily has been able to grow & transition into the Ecommerce Manager position where she primarily manages PUR & Cosmedix email marketing, online merchandising, promotional planning, and etail sales & merchandising.
