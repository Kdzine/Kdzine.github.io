International, Executive Vice President & General Manager

John LaBonty

John brings to Astral Senior executive experience in a number of industries to include consumer packaged goods, financial services and beauty. John began his career as a sales representative with Procter & Gamble and quickly progressed into management. He was recruited by Smashbox Cosmetics to be their senior executive responsible for building their global footprint outside of North America. Over a 4 year period, the international business grew nearly 500 with a corresponding increase in profitability. Most recently, he was the CEO and President of Haven Beauty, the license holder of Kardashian Beauty.
