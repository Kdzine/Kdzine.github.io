Lynnette Cole

A beauty expert and TV personality for more than 16 years, she has worked to empower women to be their best selves. Lynnette’s interest in beauty began with a personal struggle with combination and problem skin. At a very early age, she became a maverick at camouflaging imperfections and putting her best face forward. She excelled at pageantry and was crowned Miss USA in 2000. Since then, she’s also appeared in films like “Funny Valentine” and “Shattered”, on the popular daytime drama “As the World Turns,” and has hosted shows for NBC, CMT, ESPN, MTV and VH-1.
